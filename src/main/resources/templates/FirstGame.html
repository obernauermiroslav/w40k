How to play the first game?

In this game 2 battle fleets fight against each other.
You play with the Imperial battle fleet against The Chaos battle fleet.
You can choose one of your ships, then your enemy will choose one random ship and both of you will roll with virtual dice.
The ship that rolls higher number wins, and the second ship is damaged.
If damaged ship rolls again lower number, it is destroyed.
If both ships roll the same number, they are both damaged and if any of it is already damaged, it is destroyed.
He who has the last ship standing wins. You can also add or delete ships from your fleet on the main page.
