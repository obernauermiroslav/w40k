How to play the second game?

In this game you fight with your ship against enemy ships.
Your ship has shields, armor, health, and attack attributes.
If you press All hands, open fire! button, both ships will attack each other, each attack will decrease health or shields.
If you want to destroy the enemy ship, first you have to decrease shields to 0, then you must decrease health to 0.
When damaging shields, you cause full damage with your ship.
When damaging health, your attack is decreased by armor value.
If you press Brace for impact! button, both ships will also attack each other, but your ship will only suffer 40% of the damage inflicted, while its own attack power will be reduced by 40%.
If you press Focus Fire ! button, critical mod will be activated, and your ship will deal bonus damage with each attack +(8-17)
but each time you attack with this mod activated, you will loose 1SP.
Each time you destroy an enemy ship, your ship will be upgraded.
Your attributes will be upgraded, and the picture of your ship will also change.
But the enemy ship will also be upgraded, so each time you will face a stronger enemy ship.
There are also skill points(SP) that can be used for upgrading your ship.
You can use them if you have enough points to purchase an upgrade.
You can use Shield overload to recover 100 shield.
You can use Upgrade Armor to permanently upgrade armor by 3.
You can use Emergency repair to slightly repair your ship by 40 health.
You can use Upgrade Damage to permanently upgrade your attack by 3.
You can use Sabotage to decrease enemy armor or attack by 3, but it can only be used, when enemy ship has no shields.
You start with some skill points depending on the chosen difficulty(easy-15, hard-10), and for each destroyed ship, you will gain 3 more.
If you manage to destroy all enemy ships, you have won.
